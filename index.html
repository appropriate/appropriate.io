About

Appropriate Computing, LLC provides consulting services for all phases of the software development lifecycle. From design and architecture through implementation and operations, we offer both hands-on support and strategic advice to help organizations of all sizes achieve their goals.

Our mission is to enable the deployment of affordable, secure, and reliable systems that allow organizations to focus on serving their communities. We specialize in using Free/Libre and Open Source Software to build scalable systems that are easy to use and maintain throughout their useful life.

Contact

If you would like to work with us for your next project, please send an email to info@appropriate.io describing your organization and its needs. We'll follow up with you shortly to talk about how we can work together to help you achieve your goals.
